About me

I was raised in Winneconne, Wisconsin, a small town just north of Lake Butte des Morts. Growing up I was fascinated by nature, dancing, and theatre, but even more so by stories and books. I've been an avid reader from a very young age, and have always had a natural talent for writing, reading quickly, noticing grammatical errors that would often go unseen, and teaching those around me how to write professionally with a smile. I graduated as valedictorian from Winneconne highschool and am now pursuing an english degree at Minnesota State University Moorhead, while also pursuing a publishing certificate at North Dakota State University. I love exploring new places, learning about any niche topic I can find, reading whatever novel catches my eye, writing poetry, having fun with friends in unique new places, and growing in my faith as a Christian.
